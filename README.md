# AnnaSetu -- Turning Surplus Food into Lifesaving Support

🍽️ AnnaSetu – AI Powered Food Rescue Network

📌 Overview
AnnaSetu is a real-time food rescue platform that connects restaurants with surplus food to nearby NGOs and volunteers. It enables quick reporting, instant alerts, and efficient last-mile delivery, ensuring excess food reaches needy people instead of being wasted

❗ Problem Statement
Large amounts of food are wasted daily by restaurants and events
Many people still suffer from hunger and lack of food access
Lack of coordination between food donors and NGOs
Delay in food distribution leads to spoilage

💡 Solution
AnnaSetu provides a digital platform that enables:
Real-time reporting of surplus food
Instant NGO notification and response
Volunteer-based pickup and delivery
Efficient and fast food redistribution

⚙️ How It Works
Restaurant uploads excess food details on the website
System sends instant alerts to nearby NGOs
NGO accepts the request
Volunteer is assigned for pickup
Food is delivered to needy people
Impact is tracked on dashboard
